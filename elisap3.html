Simple Strawberry Shortcake$23
Ingredients: 1 quart sliced strawberries, ¼ cup white sugar, 12 ounce package prepared sponge cake dessert cups, and 7 ounce can whipped cream.
Step 1: Place strawberries in a bowl; add sugar and stir. Cover and refrigerate until sugar has dissolved.
Step 2: Place 1 dessert cup in each serving bowl and smother with strawberries. Top each with whipped cream.

Classic Vannila Milkshake$11
Ingredients: 4 large scoops of vanilla ice cream, ¼ cup milk, whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
Step 1: In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
Step 2: Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
